How to use in the cultivation of cotton and wheat

The 0.5-liter stimulator mixes with 40-50 liters of clear water and stays in 1 ton of seeds. In the phase of cotton seeding and wheat germination, mix 1 liter stimulant with 300 liters of clear water and sprinkle it with 1 sprayer on 1 GA crop area in the phase of flowering seedlings and wheat seeding tube. Sprinkle 300 liters of clear water into 1 GA crop area using a spray (OVC) Productivity increases by 14%.

Application in melon and vegetable production

Mix 0.5 liters of stimulus in 40-50 liters of clear water and weigh one ton.
